about me

hey there! it's really nice meeting you!

My name is shan mou or jenson, and I am a passionate photographer based in Melbourne. I have always had a fascination with capturing the beauty of the world around me, and my camera has become my tool for sharing that beauty with others. I specialize in capturing stories, and I love exploring new techniques and styles to bring fresh perspectives to my work. I believe that photography is not just about taking pictures, but about telling stories and capturing emotions. My goal is to create images that not only look beautiful but also evoke emotions and connect with my audience. I started my photography journey since 10, and since then, I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of clients, from individuals to businesses, and capture everything from portraits to landscapes to events. I approach every project with a unique vision and a passion for excellence, and I am always excited to collaborate with clients to bring their ideas to life.

I believe that life is all about experiencing new things and making memories, and I try to bring that same spirit of adventure and creativity to my photography.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me and my work. If you're interested in collaborating or have any stories to share, feel free to reach out!
